Frequently Asked Questions

📋 Ordering & Getting Started

How do I place an order? ▼
Click the "Start on Telegram" button. The bot guides you through selecting your category (Student, Recent Graduate, Professional, Non-Working, or Returning Client), choosing your service, and providing your information. You can upload an existing draft to save time.
Do I need a Telegram account? ▼
Yes. Telegram is free on Android and iOS. Download it from your app store, then click the button above to start the EasySuccor bot. No phone number is shared with us beyond what you provide in your CV.
Can I upload my existing CV? ▼
Absolutely! Our system extracts all information from your draft (PDF, DOCX, or image). We'll identify missing sections and only ask for what's needed, saving you significant time.
What information do I need to provide? ▼
We collect comprehensive information across 18+ categories. Below is what each category means professionally:
📌 Conferences ▼
What we mean: Industry‑specific events, seminars, workshops, or webinars that enhance your professional knowledge (e.g., Tech Summit, Medical Symposium).
What we do NOT include: Religious gatherings, social events, or general meetings without professional relevance.
📜 Certifications ▼
What we mean: Formal credentials from recognised bodies (e.g., Google Certified, TEVETA, Microsoft).
What we do NOT include: Attendance certificates without assessment or non‑accredited courses.
🌍 Languages ▼
What we mean: Proficiency in spoken/written languages (e.g., English – Fluent, Chichewa – Native).
What we do NOT include: Basic words or phrases without functional proficiency.
📁 Projects ▼
What we mean: Work‑related or academic projects with measurable outcomes (e.g., developed a mobile app, built a website).
What we do NOT include: Personal hobbies without professional or academic context.
🏆 Achievements ▼
What we mean: Quantifiable accomplishments (e.g., "Increased sales by 25%", "Won Employee of the Month").
What we do NOT include: Generic statements without measurable impact.
🤝 Volunteer Experience ▼
What we mean: Unpaid work for a non‑profit, community, or cause (e.g., Red Cross volunteer, school tutor).
What we do NOT include: Paid work or family obligations.
👔 Leadership Roles ▼
What we mean: Roles where you led a team, project, or organisation (e.g., Team Lead, Club President).
What we do NOT include: Membership without leadership responsibilities.
🏅 Awards ▼
What we mean: Recognitions from employers, institutions, or industry bodies.
What we do NOT include: Self‑declared titles or participation certificates.
📖 Publications ▼
What we mean: Articles, research papers, or books published in reputable journals or platforms.
What we do NOT include: Unpublished personal blogs or social media posts.
👥 Referees ▼
What we mean: Professional contacts who can vouch for your work (not family members).
What we do NOT include: Personal friends or relatives.
💡 Interests ▼
What we mean: Hobbies that show transferable skills (e.g., chess – strategic thinking, photography – attention to detail).
What we do NOT include: Generic likes without skill demonstration.
